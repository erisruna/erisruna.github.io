### Abstract
Then, we address the incompressible porous medium equations (IPME) that can be derived, through a suitable quadratic change of time, from the Euler equations in the case of an inhomogeneous incompressible fluid subject to an external potential. Somewhat surprisingly, thanks to the multiphasic formulation, the IPME, in spite of their "gradient flow" nature, read as a well-posed pseudo-differential hyperbolic system of conservation laws, which is confirmed by numerical experiments.
